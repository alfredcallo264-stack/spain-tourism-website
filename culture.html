Flamenco dance

A powerful mix of rhythm, guitar, and emotion—watch hands, stomps, and intensity.

Pro tip: Look for shows in Seville and Granada for classic vibes.
🍅

La Tomatina festival

Yes—tomatoes everywhere. It’s chaotic, hilarious, and surprisingly organized.

Fun fact: It happens in Buñol and is one of Spain’s most famous food festivals.
🍢

Tapas

Small plates made for sharing—perfect for students who want to try everything.

Student move: Order 2–3 tapas per person and share like a group project.
🍤

Paella

Golden rice, rich flavors, and a dish that turns dinner into a moment.

Fun fact: Traditional paella comes from the Valencia region.
🍩

Churros

Crispy, cinnamon-sweet, and best dipped into thick chocolate.

Late-night truth: Churros + chocolate is basically dessert culture.
