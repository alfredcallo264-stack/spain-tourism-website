Barcelona

Barcelona

Gaudí architecture, beach walks, and nightlife that starts late (like, very late).

Fun fact: La Sagrada Família is still under construction—so you can witness history.
Madrid

Madrid

Museums by day, tapas by night, and energy that keeps you moving.

Fun fact: Mercado de San Miguel is a “snack museum” for food lovers.
Seville

Seville

Historic charm, orange-tree courtyards, and flamenco nights that hit different.

Fun fact: Plaza de España looks like it belongs in a movie.
Granada

Granada

Alhambra magic, sunset viewpoints, and Moorish-inspired streets.

Fun fact: The Alhambra is one of Spain’s most iconic UNESCO sites.
Ibiza

Ibiza

Clubs, coves, and golden sunsets—plus lots of chill daytime exploring.

Fun fact: Ibiza is famous worldwide for its music and beach sunsets.

Student-friendly tip

Book tickets early for the big sights, then spend the rest of your day wandering—Spain rewards explorers.
